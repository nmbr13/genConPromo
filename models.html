The Models

The members of the Shadow Caste have been realized into a collection of amazingly detailed Dark Age miniatures, and the door to their underworld has creaked open, to let a few of them out into the world. The lethally overprotective Gatherers kill those who endanger anyone in their charge, while hordes of Lesser Spiderling Minions scuttle in the darkness to sink deadly poisoned fangs into their foes. Teams of Raaf hit hard and fast, turning their foes’ attacks away with one blade before opening them up with another, while the predatory Death’s Device of Shadow can withstand great punishment before felling nearly any foe with their savage swords.

Gatherers

Gatherers are nimble warriors the Shadow Caste uses to seek out new recruits from the unwanted and forgotten of Dragyri society, protecting their charges with lethal efficiency and the edge of their wicked sickle.
